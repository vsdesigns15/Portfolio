The Product

ParkEasy is a digital parking marketplace that connects drivers looking to reserve and pay for parking spaces with parking lots, parking garages, and valet services.

The Challenge

With an increase in the number of motor registrations every year, the streets are getting flooded by more and more vehicles. Moreover, finding a parking space has become a whole other task altogether.

There are a lot of public parking spaces but it is difficult to spot a place there. Parking spaces are owned by the public as well as private sectors and has become a flourishing business.

The Solution

This is more a solution for society in general. However, finding a parking lot sometimes may be a huge time consumer, so I decided to solve this by making an application in which you can reserve a parking lot within a few clicks.

There is also an option to rent your parking lot, which creates even more available parking lots.

Wireframe & Userflow

I did some research of similiar app's and wrote down some pros and cons and based on the research I decided to start with some basic sitemap & userflow which turned into a hi-fi wireframe.

The Result

I successfully put all the features that would make this application easy to use no matter the audience while keeping all elements in balance without causing any unnecessary clutter.
