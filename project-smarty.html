The Product

Smart technology has become ubiquitous at home - from your dishwasher to your TV, your smartphone to your audio system.

Any electrical appliance you can think of is likely powered by a silicon chip and growing smarter by the minute. Smarty collects all your smart devices and lets you control them with an easy-to-use application.

The Challenge

Unfortunately, smart home devices are expensive, and it's a difficult task to convince the users to switch to a whole new level of technology.

Other than that, some people are afraid to trust entirely on their smart devices. However, it can be challenging to get home automation functioning without help with some systems.

The Solution

As we all know the key to present a great product is a great looking and functional website. Since Smarty is working with huge brands like Philips, Sonos, IKEA etc.. It's easy to put some trust factors throughout the website.

However, the user should explore all the tools he might need. The versatility that the application has and ease of use, it's an excellent basis for a smart home.

The Result

I managed to showcase the options when using Smarty as the brains of your smart home. In every category of devices, Smarty works with a range of brands manufacturing exciting products. Moreover, it unifies devices from all these brands in a single app.
