The Product

A product designed to do just one thing exceptionally well: give coaches and everyday athletes a memorable workout experience. From collaborative workout programming to mobile workout-tracking, SugarWOD powers the Workout Experience.

The Challenge

Since Crossfit is a whole different sport than just a "casual fitness day", its application has other purposes and features. However, the main problem I spotted in their current product was the user experience. The web application was very unclear and hard to keep track of workouts.

However, their mobile application has different functionalities, and it was better structured. It is hard to design such a product because the target audience is between 14-56+ individuals, so the goal is to make a very balanced product and be functional.

The Solution

Redesigning completely their web application and make it more clear to the users by adding new features like enhanced workout shedule, profile, PR's, personal records, login and signup improvements.

The Result

Well strucutered web and mobile version improved with better filters and easy to navigate actions. Improved colors and contrast on the most of the sections and features. Overall the UX is improved in terms of following the daily workouts and tracking persnonal records.
