The Product

Travelloo is like Air BnB for cars, located in the United States, Canada, and the UK, is one of the peer-to-peer car-sharing networks that enable users to lend their vehicles to others.

In simple words, it lets car owners earn extra cash by renting their cars while at the same time providing a comfortable and quick way for those who need to hire a car.

The Challenge

Since there are a lot of platforms that offer car rental services, I did research, and I concluded that they lack content, character, and personality.

There was no real guide on how the user should proceed, and the experience throughout the car rental process was too confusing.

The Solution

I made environment that can be recognisible and get to know the user how the platform works and what he can expect throughout his journey to his next travel.

Sitemap and Lo-Fi Wireframe

Since the goal of any wireframe is to “fit” the elements into a layout, not indicate how elements may actually appear in a final design, only where they will be located so I decided to make a simple sitemap and low fidelity wireframe. It helps me make solid and effective structure which will benefit later in the design process.

The Result

I managed to apply successfully all insights I gathered from my research. With the current experience architecture and visual identity, this platform should bring a fresh and easy-to-navigate experience to the users.
